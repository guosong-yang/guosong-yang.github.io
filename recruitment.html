Ph.D. position available

My group is looking to fill a fully funded Ph.D. position starting Fall 2023.
We conduct fundamental research in the area of systems and control, with an emphasis on switched and hybrid systems, networked control systems, and learning in games.
We are looking for highly motivated students with a strong background in mathematics and interests in the following areas: control, dynamical systems, optimization, and game theory. Candidates are required to have a B.S. and/or M.S. degree in Electrical, Computer, Mechanical Engineering, or related areas. The application deadline is April 1, 2023.
If you are interested in applying, please send an email to guosong.yang@rutgers.edu with your CV and transcripts.
For more information on the position or our research, visit https://guosong-yang.github.io.
For information regarding the Rutgers ECE Department, visit https://www.ece.rutgers.edu.
